Socket.Kill is a real-time EVE Online killmail streaming platform. It processes kills as they happen and broadcasts them live via WebSocket — lower latency than traditional killboards.

The data comes from Squizz's newly developed R2 bucket feature, but the platform is designed to simply render and stream killmails as fast as possible, zkillboard's area is stats and history.
